AKATSUKI: ROGUE SHINOBI ALLIANCE
An alliance of powerful Shinobis who have gone rogue, each driven by their own unique goals. With their distinct red cloud cloaks, they strike fear into their enemies. Led by enigmatic figure like Pain, they seek to reshape the world according to their vision, With formidable abilities and cunning tactics, they challenge the established order, embodying the dark side of the shinobi world.
